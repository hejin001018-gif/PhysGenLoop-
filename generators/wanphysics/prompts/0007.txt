A realistic physics simulation of a car braking on a wet road.

A modern car drives on a wet asphalt road at constant velocity.

The driver suddenly applies emergency brakes.

The tires generate friction force against the road surface.
The wheels slow down and the vehicle decelerates gradually.

Water sprays from the tires.
The car travels a realistic braking distance before stopping.

The camera is fixed from the side.
The scene follows Newtonian mechanics with realistic friction, inertia and energy loss.
