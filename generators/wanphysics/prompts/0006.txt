A realistic physics simulation of a double pendulum.

Two metal pendulum arms are connected by a joint and attached to a fixed ceiling point.

The first pendulum is released from an initial angle.
Gravity creates torque and causes rotational motion.
The second pendulum follows nonlinear chaotic dynamics.

Energy continuously transfers between gravitational potential energy and kinetic energy.

The motion is highly sensitive to initial conditions.

The camera is fixed showing the entire system.
Realistic metal materials and lighting.
The simulation follows Newtonian mechanics.
