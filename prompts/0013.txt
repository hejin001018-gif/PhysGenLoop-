A realistic physics simulation of an egg falling onto a hard ceramic floor.

An egg falls from a kitchen table under gravity.

The egg accelerates downward.
When hitting the floor, the shell cracks and fragments spread outward.

The collision transfers kinetic energy into deformation and fracture energy.

Small shell pieces scatter realistically.

The camera is fixed showing the entire event.
